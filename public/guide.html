Guide to Using Product Price Tracker

Welcome to the Product Price Tracker guide. Here, you will find detailed instructions on how to use our product to its fullest potential. Follow these steps:

  1. Upload Your Excel File: Start by uploading an Excel file containing the product names and links from various e-commerce websites.
  2. View Product Details: Once uploaded, you can view detailed information about each product, including price, image, and name.
  3. Track Past Sales Data: Access historical sales data to analyze price trends over time.
  4. Price Change Notifications: Set up notifications to alert you when there are price changes for the products you're tracking.
  5. Redirect to E-commerce Page: Click on the product details to be redirected to the e-commerce site for purchasing.

For more detailed instructions and tips, please refer to our documentation or contact support.
